Poetry Helper for Claude

A little tool that helps Claude count your syllables,
find your rhymes, and perfect your verse.

Get Started

  1. Open claude.ai → Settings → Connectors
  2. Click "Add custom connector"
  3. Name it Poetry Helper
  4. Paste this URL:
https://poetry-mcp-server.elliotplant.workers.dev/mcp
5 Start writing poetry together!

That's it! Claude now has access to your poetry tools.

Try asking Claude things like:

"Help me write a love sonnet"

"Does this haiku have the right rhythm?"
